TRAINING

The physical fitness you will need to race well is something that comes over time in two very different ways. One is by sheer repetition. In other words, doing a lot of swimming, cycling and running. This stimulates your body to become an energy-generating machine. Anyone who has ever trained for a long distance triathlon knows this very well. Initially a workout lasting only a fraction of the time race day will demand can seem like an eternity. But over time, if you stay the course your physiology if nurtured properly will develop the ability to keep going farther at a high output level.
