how we set our prices

“If any of the coaches here had a business degree, or had started a coaching company efore, I'd say our pricing comes from a very specific, and complex model, but we don't have anyone here like that so we kept to the KISS rule. One thing we all know is how much time things take, and then we know the kind of work we want to do, the value of it in loving our jobs, and the monetary value of our time. Simply put, our pricing is based on to factors. The first is the value of our experience. We can teach you how to do something in one week that took us a year to figure out. Second, we strive to create relationships with our clients that will make you "a person who does triathlon (very well!)," or you can be a triathlete. We are commiitted to helping you find a balance through sport that will make you very fast, but also verty kind.”

fast

every day we start anew

What you'll get with a Fast program is:

  • A 3 month course in how to do everthing at the fundamental level correctly.
  • A training program that is tailored to your schedule, your level, and your goals.
  • A weekly checking via email with your coach (gauranteed overnight or sooner response)
  • 2 15-20 minnute calls each month if you're really struggling with something. These calls are on the "Bat Phone" and we ask everyone to respect that if you make this call we take it very seriously, like 911 seriously.
  • You have 24/7 access to your private program and profile on our website where you can see every workout, and where you and your coach can make adjstments based on the feedback you leave on your page.

The Fast Price

$169. per month with a minimum 3 month commitment
